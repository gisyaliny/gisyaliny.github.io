About Me

I am a Research Associate and GIS Programmer at the West Virginia GIS Technical Center at West Virginia University. I hold a Ph.D. in Geospatial Information Sciences from the University of Texas at Dallas (德州大学达拉斯分校). My research interests include Geographic Information Science (GIS), Urban Environment, and Human Networks Modeling. Specifically, I focus on applying geospatial big data, machine learning, and cloud computing (AWS) to study built environments, social events, semantic segmentation of urban landscapes, navigation with map matching, and more.

I enjoy developing practical applications for local communities. Check out my research for more information about my ongoing projects.
